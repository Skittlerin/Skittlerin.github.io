This is how we BREW it

At Matcha Heaven, we take our matcha seriously! Each cup is brewed with love and care, using only the finest, high-quality matcha powder sourced from the lush tea fields of Japan. We use ceremonial-grade matcha, known for its vibrant green color, smooth texture, and delicate flavor that’s both rich and refreshing.

Our brewing technique is all about creating the perfect harmony of flavors. Using a bamboo whisk  and a matcha bowl , we gently whisk the matcha with hot water in a smooth, circular motion, creating a frothy, velvety finish. The result? A cup of matcha that’s both a calming ritual and an energizing treat. Whether it’s a Matcha Latte, a fresh Matcha Lemonade, or a traditional cup of matcha, we ensure every sip is a moment of pure bliss.

We don’t just serve a drink; we serve an experience, so sit back, relax, and enjoy the magic of matcha—whisked to perfection just for you!
